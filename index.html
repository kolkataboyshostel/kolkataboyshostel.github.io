Rules & Regulations

Code of Conduct
  1. Boarders are allowed to occupy only the rooms allotted to them. No change is permissible without proper authorization.
  2. Prior to leaving the room all doors and windows should be closed, lights and fan switched off, and the water taps should be shut.
  3. The wash room taps, lights and exhaust fan to be turned off should be turned off after usage. The Warden should be informed of any complaints regarding the any hostel facilities of the hostel.
  4. Ragging is strictly prohibited in the Hostel premises. Ragging is a punishable offence under law. Any Boarder found to be indulging in ragging activity will be immediately expelled from the hostel
  5. Smoking, consumption of alcoholic drinks, use of any narcotic or psychotropic drugs is strictly prohibited. Severe action will be taken in case the boarder is found indulging in any such activities.
  6. Indulgence in any political or quasi political activities, or any activity that compromises the discipline of the hostel is forbidden.
  7. Members of the opposite sex are not allowed within the premises
  8. Proper care should be taken of personal valuables. The Hostel will not be responsible any loss or damage caused to personal belongings.
  9. No Boarder is allowed to encourage, foment, or participate in any agitation within the campus.
  10. No cooking is allowed within the hostel premises
  11. Staying outside the hostel is not permissible in any of the circumstances, but if any student wishes to stay then he has to withdraw from the hostel immediately.
  12. Boarders are strictly prohibited from spitting on walls of the room, common area and within the hostel premises
  13. Boarders are responsible to keep their room neat and clean
  14. The warden/security guard or any other person entrusted by the management can access the room as and when required.
  15. No Boarder is allowed to stay outside the hostel premises beyond 10 p.m.
  16. The hostel authority at its sole discretion can ask the boarder to vacate the premises by giving one month notice for any other reason other than on those stated above.
